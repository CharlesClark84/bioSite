Tim Clark has been a photographer for forty years. Tim has worked as a photographer for newspapers, magazines, books, websites and video games in Wisconsin, Alaska, Colorado and Tennessee. He took the photo of Eddie George for the cover of the 2001 Madden video game. Tim was the Director of Photography at Athlon Sports for 18 years. Tim also was the Photography instructor at Supreme Headquarters Allied Powers Europe for 4 years. His photographs can be found not only for sale but to look at in all forms of media. For 12 years he made calendars for the Swisher Sweet Cigar Company with swimsuit models. There are even decks of cards with the models and some other swag as well. Tim’s work can be found in an episode of Breaking Bad. Tim has published over 300 magazine covers and countless published photos to his credit with over one thousand know photo credits. With Tim’s help his son was able to get published as credited photographer in a book and a magazine.
